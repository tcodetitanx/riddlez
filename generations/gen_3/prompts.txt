GENERATION 3 - PUSHING TOWARD 40/40
====================================
Goal: Break through 35/40 ceiling to reach 10/10 in all categories

Strategy: Each mutation targets the gap between 9/10 and 10/10 in different areas.

================================================================================
MUTATION 3A: Maximum Originality
================================================================================
"Create a puzzle mechanism that has NEVER appeared in any classic puzzle.

Requirements:
- The core mechanic must be genuinely unprecedented
- Not a combination of known mechanics - a NEW mechanic
- Must still be logically valid and solvable

Possible directions:
- Temporal loops where future affects past
- Quantum-inspired superposition of answers
- Recursive meta-levels (puzzle about solving puzzles)
- New mathematical structures not yet exploited"

================================================================================
MUTATION 3B: Maximum Cleverness (Paradigm Shift)
================================================================================
"Create a puzzle whose solution changes how you think.

The 'aha moment' should be:
- Something you couldn't have conceived before solving it
- A principle applicable beyond this specific puzzle
- Counterintuitive in a way that reveals a deeper truth

Target: 'Reality bent' level of insight (Category 4 from Architecture of Puzzlement)"

================================================================================
MUTATION 3C: Perfect Simplicity
================================================================================
"Create a puzzle explainable in ONE sentence with a solution requiring 10+ minutes.

The setup must be:
- Immediately graspable by anyone
- Containing deep, non-obvious implications
- Like a well-disguised iceberg - simple surface, massive depth

Example format: 'If [simple condition], what is [simple question]?'"

================================================================================
MUTATION 3D: Hybrid Excellence
================================================================================
"Combine the best patterns from Gen 2:
1. Self-fulfilling predictions (from 2A1)
2. Constraint collapse to zero/all (from 2E3)
3. Parity forcing (from 2D1)
4. Game-theoretic equilibrium (from 2E2)

The puzzle must use AT LEAST THREE of these patterns simultaneously,
with the patterns reinforcing each other rather than just coexisting."

================================================================================
MUTATION 3E: Meta-Puzzle
================================================================================
"Create a puzzle ABOUT puzzles themselves.

Examples:
- A puzzle whose answer is 'this puzzle is unsolvable'
- A puzzle where the question is part of the solution
- A puzzle that requires understanding puzzle structure to solve
- A puzzle where you must identify what's WRONG with the puzzle

The meta-layer must add genuine intellectual depth, not just gimmick."

================================================================================
