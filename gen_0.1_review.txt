EVALUATION CRITERIA:
- Originality (1-10): How novel is this puzzle? Does it avoid being a reskin of classics?
- Cleverness of Solution (1-10): Is the solution elegant, surprising, and satisfying?
- Simplicity of Setup (1-10): Can the puzzle be explained clearly and concisely?
- Solution Success (1-10): Does the solution actually work and fully solve the riddle?

ANTI-OVERFITTING PARAMETERS:
- Trivial Rejection: Puzzles like "1+1=?" score 0/10 on Originality
- Broken Logic Rejection: Puzzles with logical inconsistencies score max 3/10 on Solution Success
- Narrative Dependency: Pure story with no logical core scores max 4/10 on Cleverness
- Accessibility Floor: If setup requires PhD-level knowledge, max 5/10 on Simplicity
